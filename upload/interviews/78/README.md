## Key Context
Donna Hatt is a passionate champion of Nova Scotia's economic development, currently serving as Economic Development Officer for the Town of Bridgewater. She has extensive experience in marketing, product development, events, and community building. She's been instrumental in exploring the opportunity for an additive manufacturing/3D printing hub centered around the Michelin presence in the Bridgewater area.

## Notable Takeaways
- Bridgewater is exploring the creation of an additive manufacturing innovation hub
- Michelin is a key anchor company with advanced 3D printing capabilities and significant cost savings
- Federal funding (ACOA) available for the initiative
- Vision includes hub as R&D, production, and learning center - not just a transactional platform
- Strong potential for ecosystem involving multiple local manufacturers leveraging 3D printing
