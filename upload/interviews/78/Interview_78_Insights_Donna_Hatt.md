# Interview 78 Insights: Donna Hatt

**Date:** March 2026  
**Interviewee:** Donna Hatt, Economic Development Officer, Town of Bridgewater, Nova Scotia

---

## KEY INSIGHTS

### 1. MICHELIN IS THE ANCHOR TENANT & KEY VALIDATOR
- **Critical Finding:** Michelin has already demonstrated proof of concept with 3D printing/additive manufacturing
- **Impact:** Saved over $500K+ in one year alone through 3D printing solutions
- **Capabilities:** Currently has 8 3D printers on-site, largest is 400mm
- **Team:** Dedicated R&D team (Carry Cool leads the printing division)
- **Examples of Impact:**
  - Parts that typically cost $5.40 now cost $0.54
  - Production of custom parts instead of $8000/day downtime (chartered aircraft scenarios)
  - Reduced downtime significantly by having on-site printing capability
  
### 2. BRIDGEWATER WANTS A PHYSICAL HUB, NOT JUST A PLATFORM
- **Donna's Vision:** Creation of a **Michelin Additive Manufacturing Innovation Hub** that is:
  - **Physical location** with R&D, production, and learning capabilities
  - **Hands-on** - not just a transactional online platform
  - **Collaborative** - businesses bringing problems, finding solutions together
  - **Trust-building** - in-person work to build confidence (not just online data)
  
- **Key Quote:** "The business world needs a closer connection because these are millions of dollars worth of equipment... We want boots on the ground - here's a lab, you're going to run this test, pop in and out, working with the folks, not just placing an order."

### 3. STRONG LOCAL ECOSYSTEM READY FOR MANUFACTURING HUB
**15+ companies already engaged / interested:**
- Mersey Seafoods (Liverpool - fish/scallops processing + shipping line)
- Alcoa
- Abcomance Lumber
- Computer systems/banks company (already using 3D printing)
- Local manufacturing base showing significant interest

**Common Problems Across Industries:**
- Supply chain delays (parts taking 7-10 days to arrive)
- High downtime costs ($100K-$1M per day)
- Need for custom parts with quick turnaround
- Inability to source locally (sourcing from US expensive/slow)

### 4. FEDERAL FUNDING PATHWAY EXISTS
- **Funder:** Agriculture and Agri-Foods Canada (AAFC) or similar federal body has allocated resources
- **Requirement:** Need solid business case demonstrating "legs" (viability)
- **Status:** Donna has already started the groundwork to secure funding
- **Next Step:** Business case development underway

### 5. CANADA MAKES IS A RESOURCE
- **Connection:** Nicole (from Canada Makes) participated in Donna's session
- **Canada Makes Role:** National clusters around innovation
- **Opportunity:** Bridgewater could be part of Canada Makes network/resources

### 6. BROADER STRATEGIC CONTEXT
- **Part of International Network:** Bridgewater is connected to 23 other Michelin cities globally
- **Innovation Hub Model:** Other Michelin cities have successfully stood up similar community innovation centers
- **Governance:** Community organizations work through mayors/elected officials

### 7. EXPANDED VISION BEYOND JUST OIL & GAS
- **Original Focus:** Mahmoud's company focused on oil & gas supply chain
- **Broader Opportunities Identified:**
  - Aerospace (confirmed high demand)
  - Defense (Tallace/Dallas contract opportunities)
  - Satellite manufacturing
  - General manufacturing (Michelin has 200+ part needs on backlog)

### 8. SUSTAINABILITY & CIRCULAR ECONOMY ANGLE
- **Full Circle Production:** Michelin demonstrates bringing composite scraps back into production
- **CO2 Reduction:** Significant carbon footprint reduction through local production vs. shipping
- **Waste Reduction:** Moving scraps back into the production cycle
- **Environmental Mandate:** Part of Michelin's global sustainability commitments

### 9. MAHMOUD'S PLATFORM VS. DONNA'S PHYSICAL HUB - POTENTIAL ALIGNMENT
**Existing Vision Difference:**
- **Mahmoud:** Cloud-based platform connecting OEMs, end users, 3D printing facilities with separate dashboards
- **Donna:** Physical hub with hands-on R&D, production, and learning center

**Potential Synergy:**
- Platform could support a physical hub
- Hybrid approach: Physical hub + digital connectivity
- Mahmoud could provide the platform infrastructure for Donna's hub vision

### 10. SKILLED LABOR & KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER OPPORTUNITY
- **NSC (Nova Scotia Community College) Involvement:** Already part of the initiative
- **Student Engagement:** Plan to have students participate in innovation (starting with trinkets, moving to real parts)
- **Knowledge Transfer:** Michelin team willing to mentor/teach others on innovations developed
- **Career Pipeline:** Opportunity to train technicians in additive manufacturing

## CRITICAL GAPS & NEEDS

- No local expertise/training available currently

### Trust & Validation
- Businesses need to see examples (Michelin is perfect proof point)
- Need hands-on experience, not just online platforms
- Skeptical of new technology

### Physical Infrastructure
- No dedicated hub/center currently exists in Bridgewater
- Need physical location for R&D, production, testing
- Equipment placement/operation requires physical space
