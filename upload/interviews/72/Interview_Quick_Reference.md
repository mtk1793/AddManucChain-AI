## EXPERT ADVICE

### On Market Focus
*"You really have to narrow in on that target market, and make sure it's as broad as possible. I've seen where the target market is such a small segment that it becomes so niche that it's hard to get any traction."*

→ **Translation:** Pick a vertical (O&G, aerospace, medical) where market is deep enough to sustain growth, but focused enough to solve a specific problem brilliantly.

### On Value Proposition
*"You really have to nail down. What is the exact problem that you're solving for the client that they can't solve in any other way? Because if it's not clear to them, what the solution is or what the problem that you're solving is, you just won't get adoption."*

→ **Translation:** Crystal clarity beats comprehensive features. If customer doesn't understand why they need you, they won't buy.

### On Canadian AM Ecosystem
*"Education. No matter how advanced the industry is... if people don't know what it is or how to use it, it's never going to gain adoption."*

→ **Translation:** Invest in customer education as core product feature, not afterthought.
