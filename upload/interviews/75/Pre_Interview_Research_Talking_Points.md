### 2. Corrosion & Material Selection Expertise
**Key topics David knows deeply:**
- **H₂S Corrosion:** Hydrogen sulfide causes stress corrosion cracking in steel
- **CO₂ Corrosion:** Carbon dioxide causes carbonic acid corrosion in steel
- **Material Selection:** Duplex stainless (25% Cr-Ni), Super-duplex (higher Cr-Ni-Mo), Specialized alloys
- **Cost vs. Risk Trade-off:** Better materials cost more but fail less
- **Subsea Materials:** Special requirements for deepwater (fatigue, fracture toughness, low temperature)

**Things to ask about:**
- What materials do you specify for high H₂S, high CO₂ environments?
- What's the cost premium for super-duplex vs. standard corrosion-resistant steel?
- Are there materials you'd use if they were more readily available?
- How do you balance material cost vs. failure risk?

### 5. Risk-Based Inspection (RBI) Management
**Topics David likely knows about:**
- **RBI Methodology:** Assess risk based on probability of failure + consequence of failure
- **Probability of Failure (PoF):** Based on corrosion rate, time in service, material degradation
- **Consequence of Failure (CoF):** Based on safety, environmental impact, economic loss
- **Inspection Planning:** Schedule inspections to detect failures before they occur

**Current challenges:**
- Corrosion prediction is often conservative (overly safe)
- Inspection schedules are time-based, not data-driven
- Lack of real-time equipment monitoring for corrosion progression

**Opportunity for AddManuChain:**
- Predictive models can forecast failures more accurately
- On-demand manufacturing allows printing parts before failure
- Direct integration with RBI workflow
