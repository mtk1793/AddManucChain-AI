## Key Context
Sebastian Piegert is the Department Lead for Additive Manufacturing at Siemens Energy, overseeing the transition of AM from R&D strategy to production-scale manufacturing. With over a decade of experience in materials science and industrial engineering, he has led Siemens' journey toward integrating AM into turbine manufacturing—including design optimization, materials qualification, and partnership models.

His recent work includes:
- **AM Forum 2026 Presentation:** "What It Really Takes: A Factory Head's Reality Check from the Turbine Shopfloor"
- **Winston-Salem Expansion:** Newly expanded North American AM center with comprehensive machining, welding, and inspection capabilities
- **Rolls-Royce Partnership:** Collaboration agreement to supply serial-production additive manufacturing parts for civil aerospace
- **Hydrogen Transition:** Leadership on incorporating AM into next-generation 100%-hydrogen-ready turbine development
