## Interview Summary

**Key Takeaways:**
- Sebastian brought 10+ years repair engineering + 12 years building AM technology team (35 people)
- Siemens Energy views distributed manufacturing as both opportunity and threat to business model
- Non-negotiable requirements for distributed AM: machine calibration, powder specs, process parameters (rest can scale)
- Post-sale service revenue is significant; spare parts availability drives OEM-customer contracts
- Supply chain resilience critical; already qualifying same parts at multiple global sites (Germany, UK, Sweden)
- Next 4 years presents major volume challenge; external vendors needed (not internal capacity alone)
- Hydrogen 100% transition introduces new IP-critical designs (will NOT be outsourced)
- Cybersecurity is essential but underestimated by most partners
- OEMs can tolerate third-party manufacturing IF quality/standards maintained AND customer relationships preserved
- Montreal facility represents on-shore North American option for distributed partnership
- MVP and proof-of-concept approach acceptable; willing to revisit partnership in 2-3 months after validation
