## Key Context
Andrew Black leads GSTS' global sales, business development, and account management team. With over a decade of experience in strategic planning and business development—particularly in AI-powered maritime and supply chain sectors—he has built deep relationships with port authorities (Port of Montreal, Port of Halifax) and pilotage organizations across Canada and internationally.

His work focuses on using OCIANA (GSTS' AI platform) to provide predictive intelligence on vessel movements, port arrivals, and supply chain optimization. GSTS has recently received ACOA funding to accelerate global deployment of OCIANA and expand into defence and security applications (NATO, Five Eyes nations).
